Vera, or the Nihilists

ACT II. SCENE.--_Council Chamber in the Emperor's Palace, hung with yellow tapestry. Table, with chair of State, set for the Czar; window behind, opening on to a balcony. As the scene progresses the light outside gets darker._ _Present._--PRINCE PAUL MARALOFFSKI, PRINCE PETROVITCH, COUNT ROUVALOFF, BARON RAFF, COUNT PETOUCHOF.

PRINCE PETRO.

(_to BARON RAFF_).

Foolish boy! [4]He will be sent into exile, or worse, if he is not careful.[4]

BARON RAFF.

Yes.[5] What a mistake it is to be sincere!

PRINCE PETRO.

The only folly you have never committed, Baron.

BARON RAFF.

One has only one head, you know, Prince.

PRINCE PAUL.

My dear Baron, your head is the last thing any one would wish to take from you.

(_Pulls out snuffbox and offers it to PRINCE PETROVITCH._)

PRINCE PETRO.

Thanks, Prince! Thanks!
